Payment Information

Secure and convenient payment options

Accepted Payment Methods

Frendi accepts credit and debit cards and transfers from your available Frendi account balance. We offer multiple secure payment options to ensure a convenient shopping experience.

Credit & Debit Cards

We accept all major credit and debit cards including Visa, Mastercard, Discover, American Express, Diners Club, and JCB. All transactions are processed securely using industry-standard encryption.

Bank Transfer

You can pay directly from your bank account using our secure bank transfer option. This method is available for both local and international customers.

Cash on Delivery

For your convenience, we offer cash on delivery (COD) service for orders within Pakistan. Pay when you receive your order at your doorstep.

Mobile Wallets

Pay using popular mobile wallet services like JazzCash, EasyPaisa, and other supported digital payment platforms for quick and secure transactions.

Security & Privacy

Your payment information is protected by advanced security measures. We use SSL encryption to ensure that your personal and financial data remains secure during transmission. We never store your complete payment information on our servers.

Important Notes

  • In some circumstances, you might be limited to using Visa and Mastercard credit cards. In those cases, the checkout process will make that clear and will let you add one of those payment methods.
  • We don't currently accept Amazon Pay, but we're working on adding more payment options in the future.
  • Frendi Gift Cards can only be used to purchase eligible goods and services on Frendi and certain related sites as provided in the Frendi Gift Card Terms and Conditions.
  • We don't currently accept Google Pay, but we're exploring options to add more digital payment methods.
  • All prices are displayed in Pakistani Rupees (PKR) and include applicable taxes.
  • For international orders, additional customs duties and taxes may apply and are the responsibility of the customer.

Refund Policy

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Some items may have different return policies, which will be clearly stated on the product page.

Need Help?

If you have any questions about payment methods, billing, or need assistance with your order, please don't hesitate to contact our customer service team. We're here to help make your shopping experience as smooth as possible.
